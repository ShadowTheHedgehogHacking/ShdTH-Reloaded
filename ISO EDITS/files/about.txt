Shadow the Hedgehog: Reloaded
by LimblessVector and dreamsyntax

It is recommended to play this ROM Hack
..on Dolphin:
....with 150% Emulated CPU Clock Override Minimum
....with Emulate Disc Speed disabled
..on Nintendont:
....with Unlock Read Speed ON

There are 4:3 and 16:9 versions available.
Do not use Nintendont/Dolphin built in "Widescreen Hacks", this ROM already properly handles aspect ratio adjustments.
